Treatments

Massage services
in Christchurch

Every treatment is tailored to you. Whether you're experiencing chronic pain, an athlete, recovering from injury, pregnant, or simply overdue for some care — there's an approach that fits.

Sports Massage

Designed for active people and athletes, sports massage targets overworked muscles, improves recovery time, and helps prevent injury. Ideal before or after events, or as part of a regular training schedule.

  • Reduces muscle soreness and fatigue
  • Improves flexibility and range of motion
  • Supports injury prevention and recovery

Deep Tissue Massage

Works through the superficial layers of muscle to reach deeper structures. Effective for chronic tension, postural issues, and areas of persistent tightness or pain.

  • Releases chronic muscle tension
  • Addresses postural imbalances
  • Helps with neck, shoulder and back pain

Relaxation Massage

A full-body Swedish massage using long, flowing strokes to calm the nervous system, reduce stress, and restore a sense of ease. No specific goal required — sometimes you just need to unwind.

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Promotes overall wellbeing

Pregnancy Massage

Safe and nurturing massage adapted for pregnancy. Helps relieve the physical strain of a changing body — particularly back pain, hip tightness, swelling, and fatigue.

  • Relieves back, hip and leg discomfort
  • Reduces swelling and fluid retention
  • Promotes relaxation and better sleep

Myofascial Cupping

A targeted technique using suction cups to lift and decompress the soft tissue, releasing fascial restrictions and improving circulation in a way that compression-based massage cannot.

  • Releases fascial restrictions and adhesions
  • Improves circulation and tissue recovery
  • Helps with chronic pain and reduced mobility

Hot Stones Therapy

Smooth, heated basalt stones are used alongside hands-on massage to warm the muscles deeply and create a profoundly relaxing experience.

  • Deep muscle relaxation through sustained heat
  • Improves circulation and eases stiffness
  • Ideal for stress relief and chronic tension

Not sure which treatment is right for you? Get in touch and Maree will help you find the best approach.

Your first session starts with a short consultation. Maree will ask about your health history, any injuries or areas of concern, and what you're hoping to get from the treatment. This helps ensure the massage is appropriate and effective for you. You'll then be left to undress to your comfort level and settle onto the massage table. Towels are used throughout to maintain your modesty.

Sessions typically run for 60 or 90 minutes. A 60-minute session is a great starting point for a focused treatment on one or two areas. If you'd like a full-body treatment with more time on specific areas, 90 minutes is recommended. You can select your preferred duration when booking online.

Yes — pregnancy massage is safe from the second trimester onwards when performed by a qualified therapist. Maree has specific training in pregnancy massage and adapts all techniques appropriately. If you have any pregnancy complications or concerns, please check with your midwife or GP before booking.

It depends on your goals. For general wellness and stress relief, once or twice a month is often enough. For active athletes or those managing a chronic condition or ongoing pain, fortnightly sessions may be more beneficial. Maree will give you a honest recommendation based on your situation.
